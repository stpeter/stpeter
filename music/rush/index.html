The Underlying Theme

Music of Rush for Solo Electric Bass

by Peter Saint-Andre

Now that I'm nearly finished with my solo bass interpretations of music by Yes, I'm thinking about doing the same thing with various songs by Rush. This project is in the earliest stages, but so far I feel that the following songs offer strong possibilities for arrangement on solo electric bass:

  • Limelight (from Moving Pictures)
  • Freewill (from Permanent Waves)
  • Losing It (from Signals)

I'll update this page as I listen to more of the music that Rush has created over the last 40 years.
