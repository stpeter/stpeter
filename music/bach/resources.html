Bach on Bass Resources

While working to learn Bach's Cello Suites on electric bass, here are some books and websites I have found helpful so far.

Books

  • Butt - Playing with History
  • Bylsma - Bach: The Fencing Master
  • Dreyfus - Bach and the Patterns of Invention
  • Gardiner - Bach: Music in the Castle of Heaven
  • Jensen and Chung - CelloMind
  • Lawson and Stowell - The Historical Performance of Music
  • Ledbetter - Unaccompanied Bach: Performing the Solo Works
  • Winold - Bach's Cello Suites: Analyses and Explorations
  • Wolff - Bach's Musical Universe

Websites

  • Bach2Bass
  • Bach Cello Suite Reviews by Mark and Charles
  • JS Bach Cello Suites by Marianne Dumas
  • Just Intonation Explained and An Introduction to Historical Tunings by Kyle Gann
