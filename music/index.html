SaintSongs: The Music of Peter Saint-Andre

I've been playing guitar, singing songs, and writing music for a long time. The music I create comes in three varieties under numerous influences: acoustic singer-songwriter fare, extended pieces for solo classical guitar with new-age overtones, and arrangements for solo electric bass.

My primary projects are as follows:

  • Fundamental Affirmation — arrangements for solo bass guitar of songs by the progressive rock band Yes (recording slated to begin in 2018)
  • Aubade — three suites and a set of variations for solo classical guitar
  • Freedom's Plow — a musical setting of Langston Hughes's long poem about America and freedom
  • Hagiography — a suite of original songs about life and love
  • RAZ — covers of some songs by Bob Dylan

Eventually I hope to also work on solo bass arrangements of music by Rush and perhaps even a recording of Bach's Cello Suites on bass guitar.
