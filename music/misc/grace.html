Part II: How Can I Thank You?

How can I thank you?
I cannot fathom good so deep
Benefaction, kindness, love
What was your fine discernment of?
I cannot fathom what I could be
What you see that you love so deep

Did I try to destroy our love?
I cannot even feel the source
Of this horror this paralysis of mine
Yet now you show what I've sought to find
That you love me, that you love me
And you say so simply "of course
Of course I love you"

How can I tell you of the beauty I see in you?
All I can do is give my thanks
For my life again for this dignity
For giving this depth of value to me
It's so far beyond forgiveness and yet
Our life can be my only thanks

Part II: Maker

My only thanks will be our life
Responsibility
Yet what destruction I have wrought
I could have been no inspiration
As you've always been to me
But never truly have you sought
Beyond our bond, outside our life

What was it drove me to destroy?
I don't know, my love
To see you build it up anew
To watch you act for our own sake
And know you act for yours
Eternal genesis from you
To hear "I love you, anyway"

Or was it fear that paralyzed
The workings of my soul?
Not really fear but puzzlement
Or awe at such an act
Incomparable no way to know
This future full with wonderment
This choice too large to analyze

Enough of gloomy deeds
I'm through with love despoiled
I'll be the one to build again
l'll be the one unflagging this time
I'll never let fear foil again
The hope of my devotion

All life's a play? Well make it good
With plot and theme and style
And character bound up with all
One whole complete alive

No cycle in pursuit of life
Straight lines of love to you
Those Greeks they'd know just what I mean
When you call my acts poetry
And I say I'm a maker
