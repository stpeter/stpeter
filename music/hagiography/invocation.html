Invocation

by Peter Saint-Andre

Noble hunter
Grey on white
Bound in the pack
Fierce alone
All at home on
Quiet ice

Wilderness animal
Of independence
Kind in heart
Of untamed soul
Wary of traps
Hungry for life

Solitude befits you
Arctic creature
Seeking forever
The true way of the wolf
Spirit knowledge
Make me whole
