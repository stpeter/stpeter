Hagiography

by Peter Saint-Andre

This suite is the story of one period of my life (thus the tongue-in-cheek title). Musically these songs are in essence folk-rock with influences from classical, jazz, blues, new age, troubadour music, and progressive rock.

In late 2006 I made some rough recordings of this music — basically sketches done live with a single mic to capture both the voice and guitar, so don't take these as definitive renditions! Over time I will produce more polished versions, but the first step is to at least document the basic idea behind each song.
