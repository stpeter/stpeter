Being Human

by Peter Saint-Andre

This book will present an integrated view of how to live an excellent human life and be a supremely accomplished person. Nothing like intellectual ambition, eh? I've sketched an outline of this book and over time will publish a series of relevant journal entries:

  • Perspectives on the Path (2018-09-05)
  • Foundations for Philosophical Practice (2018-09-24)
  • Goals of Life (2018-10-30)
  • Investing in Life (2018-11-17)
  • Impasses (2018-10-30)

Before I can write this book, as usual I have a great deal of reading and reflection to do (plus two other books to write, on Aristotle and Pyrrho).
