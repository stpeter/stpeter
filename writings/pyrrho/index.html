Gods Among Men: A Novel

by Peter Saint-Andre

This will be a novel about the Greek philosopher Pyrrho's journey to India with the army of Alexander the Great, combined with a murder mystery about Alexander's death in Babylon. So far all I've done is sketch out the merest idea of the story (see A Novel Idea and Intensive Reading), and generate a large reading list for background research.
