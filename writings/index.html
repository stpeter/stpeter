Peter Saint-Andre: Writings

Over the years I have written several books of philosophy, numerous poems and essays, and various translations from Greek, Latin, and German (not counting my technical publications).

  • Letters on Happiness: An Epicurean Dialogue (2013)
  • The Tao of Roark: Variations on a Theme from Ayn Rand (2012)
  • Randian Reflections (2009)
  • Ancient Fire: Poems and Translations (2000)
  • The Ism Book: A Field Guide to Philosophy (1996)

I have made all of these works available in the public domain, and have also started to publish them in paperback and ebook formats.

I'm currently working on three projects:

  • Adding to and collecting my Epicurus translations as a short book entitled Epicurus on the Good Life
  • Writing a cycle of philosophical poems entitled Songs of Zarathustra
  • Writing a fictional journal of intellectual exploration entitled Walking With Thoreau

After those projects are complete, I plan to translate Aristotle's Nicomachean Ethics or at least write a short book about the Aristotelian approach to eudaimonia. Eventually I might also write a book about Taoism, although I am sure that the Tao Te Ching cannot be improved upon in that regard.
