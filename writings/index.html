Peter Saint-Andre: Writings

Over the years I have written several books of philosophy, numerous poems and essays, and various translations from Greek, Latin, and German (not counting my technical publications). I have made all of these works available in the public domain.

  • Letters on Happiness: An Epicurean Dialogue (2013)
  • The Tao of Roark: Variations on a Theme from Ayn Rand (2012)
  • Epicurus Translations (2011)
  • Randian Reflections (2009)
  • Ancient Fire: Poems and Translations (2000)
  • The Ism Book: A Field Guide to Philosophy (1996)

I'm currently working on two projects:

  • Songs of Zarathustra: Nietzsche on Happiness
  • Walking With Thoreau

Eventually I plan to also translate Aristotle's Nicomachean Ethics or at least write a book about the Aristotelian approach to eudaimonia. I might also write a book about Lao Tzu if I can find the time.
