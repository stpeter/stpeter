Songs of Zarathustra

Poetic Perspectives on Nietzsche's Ethics

by Peter Saint-Andre

This book will be a set of didactic poems, intended to elucidate my perspective that Nietzsche provided a fundamentally positive approach to living. Although this genre is out of style, there's a venerable history of didactic poetry as a form of philosophical explanation; its highest exemplar is The Nature of Things by Lucretius, which describes at length the system of thought first set forth by Epicurus. Another example is the poetry of Nietzsche himself (e.g., the Dionysos Dithyrambs), little known today but very much worth reading.
