More Than Human

by Peter Saint-Andre

Let's try to bring him down to earth.
To say he has the greatest task
Or finds a way of giving birth

From chaos to a dancing star,
Unhelpfully is less than clear.
Yet we can see it isn't far
From overcoming guilt and fear

And other, all too human faults
To building up your own true soul —
To finding out a path that vaults
You ever closer to your goal.

So make yourself be something more
Than humans often deign to be;
Demand within the self-command
That turns your life to ecstasy.
