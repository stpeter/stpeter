Narrow Souls

by Friedrich Nietzsche, translated by Peter Saint-Andre

Narrow souls are what I hate:
For they are neither bad nor great.

Schmale Seelen

Schmale Seelen sind mir verhasst:
Da steht nichts Gutes, nich Böses fast.

(Translation of "Schmale Seelen", The Joyful Learning, Prelude, §18)
