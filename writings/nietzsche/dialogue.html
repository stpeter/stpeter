A Dialogue between the Wanderer and His Shadow

by Peter Saint-Andre

WANDERER
My shadow! Aren't you in my past?
I thought you were below the line:
My weaknesses, my vanity.

SHADOW
I disagree. When am I cast?
Whenever suns of knowledge shine.
I'm insight, not insanity.

(Human, All Too Human, Volume II, Part III)
