Afterword: For Further Exploration

As noted in the foreword, there is no substitute for your own first-hand encounter with Nietzsche's works.

Among the vast literature on Nietzsche's life and thought, the following is an incomplete list of books I have found to be especially insightful:

  • When Nietzsche Wept by Irvin Yalom (pure fiction, yet grounded in the meaning of Nietzsche's philosophy)
  • Nietzsche in Turin by Leslie Chamberlain (an evocative encounter with Nietzsche as a thinker and a human being)
  • Nietzsche: The Ethics of an Immoralist by Peter Berkowitz
  • ...

Songs of Zarathustra is the second volume in a six-movement suite of books I'm writing on the art of living:

  1. The Tao of Roark: Variations on a Theme from Ayn Rand
  2. Songs of Zarathustra: Poetic Perspectives on Nietzsche's Philosophy of Life
  3. A Worthy Life: The Essence of Aristotle's Ethics
  4. The Upland Farm: Thoreau on Cultivating a Better Life
  5. Letters on Happiness: An Epicurean Dialogue
  6. Stories of the Way: Parables on Taoism
