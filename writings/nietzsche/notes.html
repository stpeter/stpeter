Notes Toward Songs of Zarathustra

Allow the standard of deed
To be a rare and true relation;
By nurturing a native seed,
You feed the source of elevation.
Find courage, too, that has no need
For honors or for compensation.

What's noble is what always shows An reverent and severe devotion To higher gods that no one knows...
