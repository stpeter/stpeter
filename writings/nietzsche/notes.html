Notes Toward Songs of Zarathustra

Follow Me, Follow You

You're tempted by my style and thought?
You follow me? No, let me go!
To follow in my steps, you ought
To make your way. Go slow, go slow!

For Dancers

The smoothest ice
Is paradise
For those who chance
To know the dance.

Upwards

"What's the best way to reach the top?"
"Don't think too much, keep climbing up!"
