Notes Toward Songs of Zarathustra

Jottings

Waiting for the overman - the thinker, artist, saint -
Lacks the active principle of life and has the taint
Of slothfulness regarding the improvement of my mind
And soul. The goal, instead, is actively to seek and find

The path that only I can walk, the bridge that only I can cross,
The higher law that helps me soar so far above the dross
Of what I am, of where and when I live, ...
