My Final Cleverness

by Friedrich Nietzsche, translated by Peter Saint-Andre

So now this is my will.
Because it is my will,
All goes according to my wish.
This was my final cleverness:
I wanted what I must.
Thus I compelled each Must,
So that there is no Must for me.

(Translation of fragment 20 from Dionysus Dithyrambs)
