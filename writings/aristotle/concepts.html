Aristotle's Ethical Concepts

Here are my proposed translations for key concepts in Aristotle's ethical philosophy (contrasted with some common, often Latinate renderings):

  • arete = thriving (not "virtue")
  • boulesis = purpose
  • dianoia = thinking
  • epithumia = desire
  • ergon = work (not "function")
  • eudaimonia = living well and doing well (not "happiness")
  • hexis = settled state (not "habit")
  • orexis = striving (not "appetition")
  • mesotes = balance (not "the mean")
  • phronesis = good judgment (not "prudence")
  • proairesis = planning
  • psuche = aliveness (not "soul")
  • skopos = target
  • sophia = wisdom
  • sophrosune = moderation
  • theoria = inquiry (not "contemplation")
  • telos = goal
  • thumos = feeling
